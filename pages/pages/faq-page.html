What is Stories?

The Vision

Stories was formed with one simple objective in mind, to allow its users to discover the visceral experience of sharing tales and observations, since the dawn of mankind, knowledge has been passed on down through generations. Stories aims to revive this lost art by allowing the general public access to the untapped potential of technology in this regard.

How do i get started?

In order to get started, simply sign-up or log-in using your Facebook credentials.

What is story of the day?

Each day, out of the countless tales being recounted on this platform, one is chosen to be featured on the homepage, the decision is based on upvotes or through decision of moderators.
